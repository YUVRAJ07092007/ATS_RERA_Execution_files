### Proceedings

The matter was disposed of vide order dated 18.04.2024. The complainant filed an application dated 10.06.2024 under Section 39 seeking rectification of para 2, point 14 — from “total amount paid by the complainant” to “booking amount paid by the complainant.”

**Complainant’s submission:** Rs. 7,20,000/- mentioned in the order is the booking amount; the remaining Rs. 26,59,789/- was paid through PNB Housing Finance Ltd.

**Order:** The application for rectification is **allowed**. File be consigned to the registry.
