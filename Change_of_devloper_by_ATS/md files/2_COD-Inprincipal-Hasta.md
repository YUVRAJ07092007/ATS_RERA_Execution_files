## Reference

Your application dated 05.09.2025, 12.09.2025 & 09.10.2025 on the subject cited above.

Your aforesaid request for grant of permission for change of developer from Hasta Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. to M/s Homekraft Infra Pvt. Ltd. w.r.t. licence No. 37 of 2013 dated 03.06.2013 granted for setting up Group Housing Colony under TOD policy dated 09.02.2016 over an area measuring 11.5875 acres in sector 99A Gurugram has been considered and in-principle approval is hereby granted in accordance with the provisions of policy dated 18.02.2015 subject to the fulfilment of following conditions within a period of 90 days from issuance of this letter:

1. Fresh Agreement LC-IV, Bilateral Agreement to be executed on behalf of the new entity and bank guarantees to be furnished by the bank on behalf of the new entity against internal development works and external development charges for area measuring 11.5875 acres.

2. An undertaking to abide by the provisions of Act/Rules and all the directions that may be given by the DTCP in connection with the above said licenses.

3. To deposit balance 60% of the 25% administrative charges amounting Rs. 1,00,03,672/-.

4. That you shall submit the Registered Collaboration agreement between the proposed Developer and landowning individuals/entities.

5. That you shall clear the outstanding EDC/IDC dues, as specifically directed by the DTCP.

6. An undertaking to settle all the pending/outstanding issues, if any, in respect of all the existing as well as prospective allottees.

7. An undertaking to be liable to pay all outstanding dues on account of EDC and interest thereon w.r.t. License No. 37 of 2013 dated 03.06.2013, if any, in future, as directed by the Director, Town & Country Planning Department, Haryana.

8. An undertaking that all the liabilities of the existing Developer w.r.t. License No. 37 of 2013 dated 03.06.2013 shall be owned by new entity.

9. That you shall submit original Board Resolution of Hasta Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. to M/s Homekraft Infra Pvt. Ltd. containing specific signature of respective authorized signatory before grant of final permission.

10. That you shall get enhance the paid up capital upto Rs. 8 Crores as per order dated 14.01.2025 before grant of final permission.

11. That you re-submit the affidavit regarding creation of 3rd party rights/registration with HRERA incorporating signatures of authorized signatory of existing developers before grant of final permission.

12. The colonizer shall inform all the allottees about the proposed change of developer through publication in the leading newspapers (Two English & One Hindi) and also on his website and give time to the allottees for giving their objections to proposed change of developer to adverse effect on their rights, if any, in the office of concerned Senior Town Planner within a period of 30 days. In addition to this, the colonizer will also inform all the allottees through their e-mail ids, about the proposed change of developer. The proposal to change of developer will also be hosted on the website of the Department. After expiry of 30 days, Senior Town Planner, Gurugram will submit his report on the objections received from the third party right holders, if any received on the proposed change of developer alongwith his comments.
