# In-principle Permission for Change of Developer

## HASTA Infrastructure Private Limited

Whereas M/s Hasta Infrastructure Private Limited in collaboration with landowners, is in the process of undertaking the development of a Group housing project over land measuring 11.5875 Acs, in Sector-99A, Gurugram in accordance with the provision of License no. 37 of 2013 dated 03.06.2013 granted by the Director, Town & Country Planning Haryana, Chandigarh.

And whereas several individuals/persons have made bookings or entered into agreements for the allotment of unit(s) in the said Project.

And whereas the Director Town & Country Planning, Haryana, Chandigarh, vide memo no. LC-2806/JE(AK)/2025/46794 dated 10.12.2025 has granted its in-principle approval for Change of Developer in favour of Homekraft Infrastructure Private Limited and has directed company to invite objections in this regard.

Accordingly, vide this public notice, objections are hereby invited from allottees/public. For that purpose, a copy of the aforementioned in-principle approval dated: 10.12.2025 is available for perusal on our website, https://gurgaonsector99a.com and it can also be perused at the office of the undersigned at ATS Tower, Sector 135, Noida -201305 or at Site office or in the office of the Senior Town Planner (Gurugram), HSVP Complex, Sector 14, Gurgaon, Haryana.

Objection, if any, may be submitted in the office of the Senior Town Planner (Gurugram), HSVP Complex, Sector-14, Gurgaon, Haryana, within 30 days of the publication of this notice, failing which it shall be construed that there is no objection to the proposed approval.

**Dated:** 17.12.2025  
**Place:** Gurgaon

**Authorised Signatory**  
**Hasta Infrastructure Private Limited**
